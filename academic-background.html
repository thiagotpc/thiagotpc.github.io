Academic Background

Bachelor's in Computer Science

Coursed at Londrina State University (Universidade Estadual de Londrina, UEL) from mar/1999 to feb/2004.

During his undergraduate studies, he acquired the foundational knowledge related to Computer Science, including mathematics (discrete, statistics, calculus, and differential equations), theory of computation, formal languages, compilers, algorithms and data structures, programming language paradigms (imperative, object-oriented, functional, logical), computer architecture, databases, software engineering, computer networks, artificial intelligence (neural networks and expert systems), and computer graphics.

His final project focused on e-business, exploring the impact that e-commerce, e-ERP, e-CRM systems, among others, would have on traditional business models and the technological infrastructure required to support them.
