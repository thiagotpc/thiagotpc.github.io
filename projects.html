Projects

UUXE-ToH – uuxetoh.thiagotpc.com

UUXE-ToH (Usability and User eXperience Evaluation in Touchable Hologram) is a evaluation technology to assess user experience in touchable holographic interfaces, like solutions in Mixed or Augmented Reality.

UUXE-ToH was developed as part of my PhD research and is available as a questionnaire and as a online platform to collect and analyze data.
