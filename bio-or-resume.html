Bio or Resumè

Thiago has been an Information Technology (IT) and Internet professional since 1999. He knew MS-DOS, mIRC, Netscape, Windows 3.11, Novell Networks, ICQ, ASP and many other technologies!

Currently, he is an IT analyst at UTFPR Londrina in support, maintenance, analysis, and development activities.

He has expertise in web standards (front-end), RESTful services (back-end), information architecture, usability, and accessibility.

He has interests and researches about Software Engineering, Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), Information Systems, Information Science, and Data Science.

He has an academic background in Computer Science (graduate and master's degrees, and doctoral in progress). In your doctoral research, he studies user experience (UX) of touchable holograms with WebXR technologies. How do the users interact with your hands in Augmented or Mixed Reality? What is the aftereffect of Usability or Accessibility?

He was a coordinator and professor of the Specialization in Web Development (post-graduate course). He was also a visiting professor in other post-graduate courses (UEL, UEM, FAP-CE, Pitagoras, Univem, etc.). Furthermore, he teaches HTML, CSS, Javascript, WebAPIs, PHP, Restful, UX, Usability, Accessibility, Information Architecture, etc. For 12 years, he was an entrepreneur in a digital agency.

Occasionally, he contributes to some third-party projects. He is a working group member about Web Accessibility at the W3C Brazil office and contributes to some Ceweb.br projects. He also contributed to the Brazil project e-Gov and was a technical evangelist for Web Accessibility, Web APIs, HTML5, and CSS3! Likewise, he participated in the special committee on the accessibility of the Brazilian Association of Technical Standards (ABNT in Portuguese). He contributed to the creation of NBR 17060:2022 on accessibility requirements for mobile devices.
